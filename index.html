الأسماء الحسنى - The 99 Names of Allah

This is a memory game to learn the 99 names of Allah.

  • In Islam, Allah has 99 names or attributes, each reflecting a distinct aspect of His nature.
  • The Qur'an emphasizes these names:
    اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ لَهُ الْأَسْمَاءُ الْحُسْنَىٰ
    "Allah – there is no deity but Him – the most excellent names belong to Him." (Sura Ta Ha, Verse 8)
  • Learning these names aids believers in understanding Allah's qualities and the spiritual teachings in Islam.
  • This game invites both Muslims and non-Muslims to explore and engage with the 99 names of Allah in an interactive way.
  • You can choose from three levels—easy, medium, and challenging—by pressing the buttons below.
  • This game follows the basic rules of a classic memory game. All cards are on the table face down.
  • A random generator will choose a pre-defined number of names. Clicking on a card will turn it over and display one of Allah's names.
  • You have to find all pairs of cards to finish the game.
  • Two counters will keep track of both your score and your attempts.
